About Us

Establish a style of learning that is stress-free and impactful.
Provide a compelling education alternative through a powerful network of institute partners.

Overview

There is a $15 Billion dollar opportunity that Uprep is solving today.

Stressful environment in factory style coaching or lack of standardized delivery of content in local coaching institutes is a major problem faced by students today. Disconnect between classroom Pedagogy and after-class support options like online Tutoring , Self-Learning Apps and Tuitions is the bane of the education industry.

UPrep is led by a team of spirited young professionals who firmly believe that education, driven via technology, will bring about the next wave of growth in the country.

The Problem

High-potential students unable to Optimize their Learning with the available coaching institute options

The Solution

UPrep will make learning personalized for every student within and outside the classroom and optimize their learning potential.
UPrep will uberize coaching institutes across India by providing access to students, technology and impactful pedagogy

Present traction
Rs. 16 Lakhs revenue
In just 2 months!
Technology and content ready!
