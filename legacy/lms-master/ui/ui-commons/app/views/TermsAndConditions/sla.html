Learnpedia Products for Business Service Level Agreement

During the Term of the applicable Learnpedia Products for Business Agreement (the "Agreement"), the Learnpedia Products Covered Services web interface will be operational and available to Customer at least 99.9% of the time in any calendar month (the "Learnpedia Products for Business SLA"). The following definitions shall apply to the Learnpedia Products for Business SLA.

  • "Downtime" means, for a domain, if there is more than a five percent user error rate. Downtime is measured based on server side error rate.
  • "Learnpedia Products for Business Covered Services" means the Learnpedia Admin Tool and Learnpedia Learn Platform.
  • "Monthly Uptime Percentage" means total number of minutes in a calendar month minus the number of minutes of Downtime suffered in a calendar month, divided by the total number of minutes in a calendar month.
  • "Service" means the Learnpedia Products for Business service provided by Learnpedia to Customer under the Agreement.

Learnpedia Products for Business SLA Exclusions. The Learnpedia Products for Business SLA does not apply to any services that expressly exclude this Learnpedia Products for Business SLA (as stated in the documentation for such services) or any performance issues: (i) caused by factors described in the "Force Majeure" section of the Agreement; or (ii) that resulted from Customer's equipment or third party equipment, or both (not within the primary control of Learnpedia).
