Our Products

SCORE (Simple and Complete Online Resource) is a digital learning platform for various competitive exams. Currently two versions of SCORE – ScoreJEE and ScoreNEET, are available.

ScoreJEE is a digital test prep platform for students preparing to appear for the Joint Entrance Exam for admission into the prestigious IITs (Indian Institutes of Technology) and NITs (National Institutes of Technology).

ScoreNEET is a digital test prep platform for students preparing to write the Pre Medical Tests for admission into prestigious medical schools across the country.

What We Do

Learnpedia ploughs through your entire curriculum meticulously for you and presents it in a simplified and interesting format.
At this virtual classroom, the syllabus takes the form of stimulating e-books, engaging video lectures and 3D animations. From getting your doubts answered and joining study groups to assessing yourself using special performance management tools, you can do it all from the comfort and convenience of your home. All you have to do is log on, study smart and score big.

Presently, two variants of SCORE are available - ScoreJEE for engineering aspirants and ScoreNEET for medical aspirants.

Besides this, Learnpedia also helps educational institutions like Schools/Colleges/Coaching Institutes to enhance the learning experience of their students by complementing their classroom delivery with our content and technology. To know more, please get in touch with us at info@learnpedia.in
