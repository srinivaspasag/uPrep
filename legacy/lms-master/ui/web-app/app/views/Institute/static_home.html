About Lakshya

Lakshya is a dream child of four young IIT graduates conceptualized with an aim to revolutionize the way learning is perceived and teaching is delivered. Lakshya institute is currently started up in the very tangible segment of IIT-JEE, AIEEE and medical entrance examinations training where they train students for these competitive examinations which they give after their XII std.

Upcoming Features on Institute page:

  • Teachers and Students profile: There will be institute based profiles of teachers and students
  • Discussion Forum: Teachers and students will be able to engage in institute based academic discussions through a comprehensive discussions interface
  • Content Sharing: Teachers would be able to share specific content with their students
  • Institute's Library: Access to official institute content
  • Curriculum management: Access to features like Class schedule management and Course tracking
  • Ask Expert: Students will be able to post direct questions (Academic/Non-Academic) to various experts/Teachers
