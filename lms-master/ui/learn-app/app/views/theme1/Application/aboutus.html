About Us
Learnpedia is an edu-tech company that promises to make learning a fun and fascinating experience for students preparing to write various competitive exams. Its flagship products ScoreJEE & ScoreNEET are already redefining preparation for JEE & NEET.Headquartered in Hyderabad, Learnpedia is promoted by seasoned academicians with deep rooted understanding of the test-prep industry and a compelling passion to nurture next-gen talent. It aims to bridge all the gaps in traditional competitive exam preparation by providing engaging and interactive digital content.
Vision
"To be a leader in providing Tech enabled futuristic educational solutions to maximise student potential and build successful careers"
