CLASSROOM CONNECT

Problem

  • Over 30 lakh students appear for JEE, NEET exams every year. These students and their parents have a wide choice of national-level and local coaching institutes to pick from. However, the students are either stressed out in the factory-style coaching run by the big names or are hindered by the lack of a standard, well-rounded pedagogy in the local institutes.

  • After-class preparation plays a crucial role in acing exams such as JEE, NEET. There is a stark disconnect between the classroom pedagogy and the various alternatives that students have for after-class preparation - the likes of tuitions, self-learning apps - thereby hindering students from optimizing their learning potential.

Solution

  • UPrep is India’s first integrated learning platform for students that brings classroom coaching and self-prep together. UPrep empowers coaching institutes and tutors with access to students and technology, to enable the highest quality of delivery for impactful learning outcomes.

  • With UPrep, we are redefining classroom to optimize learning. We equip the classroom with standard pedagogy, stress-free environment, provide students with personalized learning through app. We are working closely with our partner institutes to deliver a top-notch classroom experience for students with the help of technology. Our partner institutes benefit from our association in the form of increased enrollments, better academic results, and not having to work on their non-core activities viz. Customer acquisition, marketing etc. and instead focus on delivering their best inside the classroom.
